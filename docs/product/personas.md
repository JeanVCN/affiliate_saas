# Personas

## Affiliate Achadinhos Creator

Needs daily product ideas, fast copy generation, clean link organization, and channel-specific posting packages.

Pain:

- Links scattered across notes and chats.
- Hard to know which product deserves attention.
- Hard to measure which post generated sales.

## Review And Comparison Affiliate

Creates product reviews, comparisons, and evergreen content.

Pain:

- Needs accurate product facts.
- Must avoid false claims.
- Wants reusable SEO/social assets.

## Deals Page Operator

Runs Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, Telegram, or YouTube Shorts pages focused on offers.

Pain:

- High volume.
- Repeated manual copy work.
- Needs product and channel performance ranking.

## Small Agency

Manages multiple pages, clients, or niches.

Pain:

- Needs approval workflow.
- Needs reports.
- Needs reusable brand and compliance templates.

## Small Business With Affiliate Program

Future persona. Wants affiliates to promote its products with approved assets and compliant copy.
